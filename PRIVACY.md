# Privacy Policy — ScreenQuest (FocusFriend)

**Last updated: March 13, 2026**

## Overview

ScreenQuest ("the App") is a focus timer app that helps you build productive habits through gamification. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.** All app data stays on your device.

Specifically:
- **No analytics or tracking** — We do not use any analytics SDKs or tracking pixels.
- **No network requests** — The app works entirely offline. No data is sent to any server.
- **No account required** — There is no sign-up, login, or user account system.
- **No third-party services** — We do not integrate any third-party data services.

## Data Stored on Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device using UserDefaults:
- Focus session history (dates, durations, labels you choose)
- Game progress (XP, level, quest completion, skill unlocks)
- Streak tracking data
- Your notification preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications (timer completion, streak reminders). These are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any server communication.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy if app functionality changes. Updates will be reflected in the app and this document.
